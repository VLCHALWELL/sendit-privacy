Privacy Policy

SendIT · Last updated: April 2026

SendIT is a personal file transfer app that sends files between your Android device and your own Mac. Your privacy is straightforward: we collect nothing.

What data does SendIT collect?

None. SendIT does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party.

How does file transfer work?

Files are transferred directly between your Android device and a server you run on your own Mac, on your own network. The developer has no access to your files, your server, or your network.

What is stored on your device?

SendIT stores only your server URL and password locally on your device (in the app's private storage). This information never leaves your device except to connect to your own server.

Third-party services

SendIT does not use analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or any other third-party SDKs that collect data.

Children's privacy

SendIT is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
